Alternative Formulation of BDF Schemes

The Taylor Series approach to deriving the weights for the BDF schemes gets much more difficult if not imposible when the points are not equally spaced. Another approach using polynomial interpolation generalizes and doesn't have this shortcomming.

Given a point $t_0$ at which we would like to approximate the derivative, and previous points $t_{-1}, t_{-2}, ..., t_{-n+1}$ along with the known function values at these previous points $u_{-1}, u_{-2}, ..., u_{-n+1}$, we first construct the polynomial interpolant through all $n$ points. Differnetiating the interpolant and evaluating at $t$ gives us the $\mathcal{O}(n)$ approximation to $u^\prime (t_0)$.
